External & Internal Windows Cleaning

Brightech is a market leader in external washing systems applications. With Brightech, customers can now experience the latest pure water technology firsthand for keeping external surfaces and windows cleaner, brighter and sparkling for longer periods. This comes without the hassle of scaffolding, man-lifts or rope access teams, as the “Carbon Fiber” pole system can reach up to 18 meters outdoor and 11 meters indoor windows and facades.

Pure water technology

- Unlike many outdated washing solutions in the market, pure water brings so much value to the industry.
- With a zero mineral content, pure water does not leave any marks on your windows or surfaces. Our system filters any form of water into pure water, which is then used for external cleaning.

Innovative

Our external washing system is mobile and self-contained, allowing us to always come fully prepared on site, with everything we need, no matter the complexity of the job. It utilizes lightweight carbon fiber poles which, when attached, allows us to reach heights up to 18 meters for our external surface washing.

Efficient

We strive to bring the latest solutions to the market, as long as they prove effective in solving our clients’ needs. Results have shown that our pure water technology system cleans faster than any available competition. We do not use detergents and our pure water has no mineral content, thus it eliminates the need to wipe dry surfaces, giving a longer lasting sparkle to your windows and surfaces.

Surface cleaning

Showrooms, low rise buildings (palaces, villas and hotels) with high windows or smooth façade, are our area of expertise. We also cover solar panels, large vehicles and yachts.

Eco-Friendly

The Earth is what we all have in common. At Brightech, we are determined to have our cleaning systems as eco-friendly as possible. With pure water, we avoid the need to use plastic or bottled chemicals, cleaning agents, or solvents on our clients’ surfaces. We just use pure water!
