Although Brightech does not engage in house maid services, we do provide a variety of cleaning and disinfection specialty services for your home. Our highly trained cleaning professionals will deep-clean your property top to bottom, using latest eco-friendly cleaning products, to ensure that your family has a healthy environment. As in spring cleaning, we recommend that our clients conduct routine deep cleaning to maintain continuous protection and a clean home.

These services include:

- Deep cleaning of rooms, bathrooms and kitchen
- Internal and external window cleaning
- Furniture & mattress deep cleaning and shampoo
- Carpet cleaning and shampoo
- Villa external cleaning
- Grout care / pressure washing
- Full home disinfection
-Vehicle disinfection
- Pre move-in or post move-out cleaning and disinfection

We offer peace of mind to our clients by using disinfectants that are approved by the Dubai Municipality, 100% safe around children and pets, and handled by professional cleaning operatives.
