Covid-19 Disinfection Services

The recent COVID-19 outbreak has forced many challenges to businesses and households alike. As we approach a positive shift in the infection curve, organizations will begin to get back to normal business, but not without the hygiene changes that protects their staff. There will be requirements for healthier workplaces, and standards put forth by governing bodies will require higher frequency in coronavirus disinfection services.

Since the beginning of the outbreak, Brightech has made it a top priority to keep up to date with the knowledge and principles which are applicable for health, safety and disinfection. This was done through extensive training for our management and staff, learning latest published guidelines and articles from governing bodies, and acquiring coronavirus control certifications to our already existing certification base.

Commercial Disinfecting services

Brightech is proud to be an approved disinfecting agent by the Dubai Municipality, and we look forward to supporting you in keeping your customers and employees safe at the workplace.

In addition to our usual Commercial cleaning services, we now include disinfection with the following:

- Hospital Grade Disinfectants – EPA registered and Dubai Municipality approved
- High Touch Point Focus
- Technologically Advanced Chemical Dispersal Systems
- Public Health & Safety Guidelines Compliant
- Recurring Services Available*

*Different industries have different requirements, however the rule for routine disinfecting services is as follows:

Residential Disinfecting Services
