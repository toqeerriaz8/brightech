About Us

Brightech is passionate about being a leading provider of the highest quality of cleaning services while utilizing the latest technology in the industry along with environmentally responsible cleaning products. We aim to maintain our top professionalism and quality, by providing highly trained staff devoted to service our clients while retaining our competitive price edge.

We adapt to our customers’ needs with excellent support from a dedicated management and staff team to ensure the continuous satisfaction of our clients. And with our franchiser, Mega Maid Abu Dhabi, we have built a list of highly satisfied customers over the past 11 years.
