Providing cleaning and disinfecting services for the commercial sector is the focus and heart of our business. The productivity in your workplace depends upon the health and morale of your staff, and we at Brightech prioritize our efforts into making your workplace safe and clean for them to optimize productivity.

Our services can be contracted annually, monthly or as per clients’ requirement, they include:

- Full site cleaning & disinfection (floors, furniture, fixtures, carpets, restrooms and windows)
- Janitorial services
- External window / façade cleaning
- Furniture deep cleaning
- Carpet deep cleaning and shampoo
- Hard floor and grout care
- Pressure washing
- Vehicle / Bus deep cleaning disinfection

As we are an approved agent of Dubai Municipality, we offer daily, weekly and monthly disinfection and sterilization services, all within the strictest health and safety guidelines of the Municipality.
