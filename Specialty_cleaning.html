Deep / Specialty cleaning

At Brightech, customized and specialty cleaning is our forte. Whether you are preparing for a special event, moving into a new home or office, required to clean vacated premises, or can’t get that hard stain off your floor, carpet or furniture, we provide the solution.

We can tackle any stubborn cleaning issue, be it a stain, grout build up, rust, glue, etc., which other cleaning companies shy away from.

Some of our “Specialty” cleaning services include:

- Post construction of villas, apartments and buildings (general cleaning) - Post-delivery deep cleaning (bathrooms, doors, wardrobes and other wooden fixtures)
- Move-in / move out cleaning
- Tile / marble cleaning and grout removal
- Pre and post events cleaning
- Metal polish (hand rails, stainless steel fittings)
- Carpet / furniture deep cleaning and shampoo
- Power washing of external areas
- Yacht interiors
